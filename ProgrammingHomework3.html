SUMMARY

The Phanton Menace

Unrest begins in the Galactic Republic due to protests against the taxation of trade routes to star systems, and a nascent separatist movement. The Trade Federation has stopped all shipments to the planet of Naboo, led by Queen Padmé Amidala, an ally of Senator Palpatine. The Jedi Qui-Gon Ginn and his Padawan, Obi-Wan Kenobi, protect the young Queen Amidala, who escapes an invasion of her planet, with the intention of reaching the Senate and making her case. During this adventure, on the planet Tatooine, the group meets a slave boy named Anakin Skywalker, who meets the characteristics to be a being that, according to an ancient prophecy, will bring balance to the Galaxy. Qui-Gon Jinn frees Anakin from his chains and sets out to train him as a Jedi; the little boy must be separated from his mother, who remains a slave on Tatooine. During the battle to free Naboo from illegal occupation, the Sith Darth Maul assassinates Qui-Gon Jinn, but is subsequently defeated by Obi-Wan Kenobi. Anakin is put in the care of Obi-Wan Kenobi and Naboo is freed; the conflicts between the Republic and the separatists do not cease

Attack of the Clones

10 years have passed; Anakin is now a young adult and Padmé Amidala went from queen to senator. Thousands of solar systems have declared their intentions to leave the Republic, which creates tension in the Galactic Senate, a situation that has given strength to the separatist movement, led by the mysterious Count Dooku. The limited number of Jedi Knights do not have the strength or the resources to guarantee order and peace in the galaxy. Legislators must vote on the possibility of creating a Republic Army, the purpose of which will be to assist the Jedi in their surveillance and security activities. Upon reaching the Senate, Padmé Amidala is the victim of a terrorist attack, and although she survives, fear for her well-being leads to Anakin Skywalker being assigned to her care; the two young men begin to fall in love. Anakin returns to his home planet in hopes of meeting his mother; there he discovers that she was kidnapped by a tribe of savages. Anakin throws himself to the rescue of his mother, who dies in the arms of the young Jedi, victim of the mistreatment and torture to which she was subjected. Furious, Anakin kills all the members of the aforementioned tribe, starting his way to the Dark Side of the Force. Meanwhile, Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin's teacher, discovers that the Republic is already working on creating an army of clones (stormtroopers) to ensure safety. At the end of the movie, Obi-Wan and Anakin dove for Count Dooku's head, but are outnumbered; reinforcements soon arrive: members of the newly integrated Republic Army and Yoda, one of the Jedi leaders. Dooku escapes and the Galaxy enters an all-out war, and Anakin and Padmé secretly marry.

Revenge of the Sith

The attacks and attacks of Count Dooku and the separatists do not stop; One of its best elements, the droid leader General Grievous, enters the Republic capital and kidnaps the now Supreme Chancellor Palpatine, leader of the Senate, so Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker lead a mission to rescue him. During the operation, Anakin murders Count Dooku in cold blood, and becomes close to Chancellor Palpatine, who throughout the film urges the young Jedi to join the Dark Side. Padmé is pregnant, and Anakin worries about her well-being; Because he also considers that the Republic does not protect the victims of abuse, as was his mother, the boy ends up being convinced that the democratic system is out of date. Palpatine is revealed as the Sith Darth Sidious, and the Jedi attempt to stop him, however, they are betrayed by Anakin, who joins the dark side. Darth Sidious harnesses his power as the leader of the Republic and orders the Army to assassinate all Jedi, including the children who were trained (which Anakin personally takes care of) to end the lineage of the light side of the Force. Driven mad, Anakin mortally wounds his beloved Padmé and confronts his former teacher, Obi-Wan. Obi-Wan Kenobi defeats Anakin and after giving him up for dead, he abandons him to his fate in a river of lava; Darth Sidious searches for his new student, saves him, and turns him into the fearsome Darth Vader. Padmé dies giving birth to two twins, Leia and Luke, who are separated: the girl is placed in the care of Senator Bail Organa, and Luke is sent with Obi-Wan Kenobi's brother, Owen Kenobi.The Sith triumph, the Republic falls, and the Galactic Empire arises.
